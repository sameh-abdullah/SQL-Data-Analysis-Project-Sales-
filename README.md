# 📊 Project Overview

This project is a comprehensive data analytics using SQL server designed to transform raw sales, customer, product, and logistics data into actionable insights. By integrating multiple datasets—such as orders, customers, products. the project enables stakeholders to monitor performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🎯 Business Roles & Their Objectives + Questions

## 👔 1. Sales Manager

**Objective:** Increase revenue and monitor sales performance

**Key Questions:** - What are total sales by month, quarter, and year?
- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- Which customers contribute the most to sales?
- What is the average order value?
- Are there seasonal trends in sales?
- Which regions (countries) perform best?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 📊 2. Marketing Analyst

**Objective:** Understand customer behavior and improve targeting

**Key Questions:** - Who are our most valuable customers?
- What is the distribution of customers by gender and country?
- Do certain customer segments buy specific product categories?
- What is the customer purchase frequency?
- Are there patterns based on age (from `birth_day`)?
- Which products are popular among different demographics?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 📦 3. Product Manager

**Objective:** Optimize product portfolio and profitability

**Key Questions:** - Which product categories and subcategories perform
best?
- What is the profitability per product? (`sales` vs `product_cost`)
- Which products have low sales and may need to be discontinued?
- How do different product lines perform over time?
- Are new products (based on `start_date`) performing well?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🚚 4. Operations / Supply Chain Manager

**Objective:** Improve delivery efficiency and reduce delays

**Key Questions:** - What is the average shipping time?
(`ship_date - order_date`)
- Which orders have the longest delivery durations?
- Are there delays by product or region?
- How does delivery performance impact sales?
- Are certain periods experiencing slower shipping?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 💰 5. Finance Analyst

**Objective:** Track revenue, cost, and profitability

**Key Questions:** - What is total revenue vs total cost?
- What is profit margin by product/category?
- Which customers or products are least profitable?
- How does revenue trend over time?
- What is the contribution margin per product?
